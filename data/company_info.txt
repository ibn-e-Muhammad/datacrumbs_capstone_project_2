# Lumiere Health Technologies - Company Information
Last Updated: June 2026

## About Us
Lumiere Health Technologies is a pioneering health-tech company founded in 2018, dedicated to democratizing access to preventative healthcare through intelligent wearables and AI-driven insights. Our headquarters is located in Austin, Texas, with satellite offices in London and Singapore.

## Core Mission
To empower individuals with actionable health data, fostering a proactive approach to well-being that extends lifespans and enhances life quality.

## Employee Policies
- **Remote Work**: We operate on a flexible hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office Tuesday through Thursday, with Monday and Friday available for remote work. Fully remote positions are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
- **Vacation**: We offer a generous "Take What You Need" unlimited PTO policy, with a minimum requirement of 15 days taken per year to ensure employee rest and mental health.
- **Health Insurance**: Full coverage for medical, dental, and vision is provided for all full-time employees and their dependents through Blue Cross Blue Shield.
- **Wellness Stipend**: Employees receive a $100 monthly stipend for gym memberships, wellness apps, or fitness equipment.
